About ZeroTrace

ZeroTrace is a cybersecurity community focused on competitive hacking, knowledge sharing, and skills development through Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions. Our members range from beginners curious about cybersecurity to experienced professionals looking to sharpen their skills.

CTF Focus Areas

  • • Web Exploitation
  • • Reverse Engineering
  • • Binary Exploitation (Pwn)
  • • Cryptography
  • • Forensics

Our Community

  • • Inclusive & Supportive
  • • Knowledge Sharing
  • • Regular Events
  • • Mentorship Programs
  • • Global Network

Our Mission

To build a collaborative environment where cybersecurity enthusiasts can learn, compete, and grow their skills while forming connections with like-minded individuals from around the world.
